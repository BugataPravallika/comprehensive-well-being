Predicting Global Human Well-Being

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a summary measure of average achievement in key dimensions of human development: a long and healthy life, being knowledgeable, and having a decent standard of living.

This ML-powered prediction dashboard uses a Linear Regression model to predict the HDI score of a country based on major socioeconomic indicators. Enter indicators manually, auto-fill from country records, or run side-by-side comparative analyses.
